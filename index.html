About me

I am currently a second-year student at the University of Ottawa, studying Software Engineering with an option in Engineering Management and Entrepreneurship. With a passion for programming and technology, I’ve gained hands-on experience through a recent CO-OP placement at Cisco where I automated various router processes using Ansible. In the past, I had an internship where I worked as a Full Stack Developer enhancing website designs and functionality. Through my academic career, I’ve become proficient in Python and Java. Additionally, I was involved in a school project, in which I collaborated with my teammates to create a marketing website (HTML, CSS) and a pitch for our product. I am known for being a hard worker, meticulous, creative, organized, and I am always willing to learn. Along my journey, I aspire to further enrich my programming skills, expand my network, and explore new opportunities in the tech industry. I also enjoy photography, baking and traveling!
