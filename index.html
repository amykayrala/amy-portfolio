About me

I am currently a second-year student at the University of Ottawa, studying Software Engineering with an option in Engineering Management and Entrepreneurship.

With a passion for programming and technology, I’ve gained hands-on experience across multiple roles at Nokia, Cisco and as a Full Stack Developer at Digitera.

Through my academic career, I’ve become proficient in Python, Java, HTML, CSS through projects and assignments.

I am known for being a hard worker, meticulous, creative, organized, and I am always willing to learn. Along my journey, I aspire to further enrich my programming skills, expand my network, and explore new opportunities in the tech industry.

I also enjoy photography, baking and traveling!
